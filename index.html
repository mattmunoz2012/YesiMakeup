About Yesica

Artistry Rooted in
Passion & Precision

I'm Yesica — a Chicago-based makeup artist who believes beauty is one of the most personal forms of self-expression. Every face I touch is treated as a canvas, every brushstroke intentional. My work is a collaboration between technique and feeling, between what looks stunning on camera and what makes you feel undeniably yourself.

With 5 years of professional experience across bridal, editorial, film, and high-profile events, I bring both technical mastery and genuine artistry to every appointment. I work with all skin tones, all skin types, and all visions.
